A Few Things People Ask

Why does this open my email app instead of just sending?
creative-pages.org runs as a simple static site — no server sitting behind it collecting form data. The "Open in Email" button builds a pre-filled message and hands it to your own email client, so nothing gets lost in a spam filter or a database somewhere.
How long until I hear back?
Usually within 2–3 days. Email lands straight in the inbox at admin@creative-pages.org — no auto-responder, no ticket queue, no AI gatekeeper reading it first.
Do you take on small or one-off projects?
Yes — anything from a single landing page or a domain/email cleanup to a full brand strategy. If it fits one of the four categories on the Work page, it's worth sending.
What does "planned by human, perfected by AI" actually mean for my project?
The brief, the strategy and every creative decision come from a real conversation with you. AI is used afterward to accelerate the build — code, copy refinement, layout iteration — so the work moves faster without losing the human judgment behind it.
