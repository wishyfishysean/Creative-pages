The Process

The Molting Log

A record of how a thing becomes a slightly larger version of itself, one uncomfortable stage at a time.

STAGE 01

The Splitting

A seam opens along the back. There is no good way to describe this stage. We've tried several times.

STAGE 02

The Emergence

What comes out is soft, pale, and unfinished — a draft of the final thing, still deciding on its shape.

STAGE 03

The Hardening

Over several hours, the new exterior sets. This is the only stage that looks remotely peaceful.

STAGE 04

The Leaving

The old shell stays exactly where it was — a perfect outline of something that used to be there, and now isn't. That's what we collect.
